Admission Process & Eligibility

Step 1: Submit Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal and academic details.

Step 2: Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of your academic transcripts, passport, and other required documents.

Step 3: Receive Admission Letter

Once your application is approved, you'll receive an official admission letter from the university.

Step 4: Apply for Student Visa

Apply for a student visa at the nearest embassy or consulate of your chosen country.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% in 12th grade (Science stream).
  • Valid NEET score (for Indian students).
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL if required).

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for admission?

You need your academic transcripts, passport copy, NEET scorecard (if applicable), and a statement of purpose.

How long does the admission process take?

The admission process typically takes 4-6 weeks, depending on the university.
